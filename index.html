About

I'm Liam — I get to play with upcoming tech and turn it into things that feel good in your hands and on your face. Spatial computing, smart glasses, computer vision, machine learning — anything that gives me a new toy to figure out. I co-founded ohi Studio, and I also consult with teams building in this space.

Before that I did a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Lincoln, and taught game design, HCI, and games programming as an associate lecturer. My doctoral research was on playful technology for wheelchair users — work that ended up at CHI, the top human-computer interaction conference.

I like prototypes you can actually hold, problems that don't have one right answer, and the moment when a piece of tech crosses over from "neat" to "I want one." Always poking at what's next.
